My name is Sira Barry

As a Graphic Designer, I specialize in crafting compelling visuals and branding that enhance user engagement and brand identity. My proficiency in tools like Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Canva, Pixellab, and Inkscape) and Figma allows me to create seamless digital experiences.

Beyond development and design, I am an IT Instructor dedicated to sharing knowledge and empowering aspiring tech professionals. I have experience teaching programming languages, web development, and IT fundamentals, helping students and professionals build their careers in the tech industry.
